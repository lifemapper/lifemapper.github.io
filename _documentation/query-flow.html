Querying the contents of a Lifemapper installation

Aggregate queries will give you a sense of the size, depth and breadth of a Lifemapper public archive.

To query input and output objects by time and status, use the following Get operations to count or list objects that meet the filter criteria: [Get OccurrenceSets](/documentation/api.html#!/Occurrence_Sets/get_sdm_occurrences_format)
